About Me

Hi! I’m Shandelle Brown and I’m a Front End Developer living in Dallas, TX. I work full time for an awesome company called Kalkomey. We build online learning websites like Boat-ed.com and Hunter-ed.com. I’m a lifelong learner that loves traveling, trying new foods, and weenie dogs. I sort of fell into coding when I worked for an amazing company in St. Louis called Vector Communications. There, I was encouraged to take up something that would help to develop me professionally. I previously worked through a few courses on Codecademy, so I started looking into ways that I could really start learning to code. After moving to Dallas, I discovered Tech Talent South and really jumped right in with them. I signed up for the full—time bootcamp and the rest is history! I found my love for code and plan to keep following it wherever it may lead me.

When I’m not working, I’m blogging at Fleur de Vino, watching movies, messing around on CodePen, playing with my dogs, or trying out the endless supply of restaurants that Dallas has to offer. I’m excited for what the future holds and especially the coffee yet to be tasted!
